Unreal Engine Projects: Case Studies in Real-Time 3D Game Development

College of Arts, Media, and Technology, Chiang Mai University

Research Project Dossier on Student-Led Commercial Entertainment Deployments

This dossier details commercial-grade game productions built using Epic Games' Unreal Engine within the academic pipelines of Chiang Mai University. It showcases the technical capacities, visual workflows, and publication pipelines managed entirely by student developers, bridging the gap between academic research and international entertainment platforms.

II. FEATURED STEAM RELEASE

Developed as a culmination project at Chiang Mai University, Whisper of Anima is a high-fidelity fantasy action-adventure game. The project demonstrates full-scale studio integration, combining advanced graphics pipelines, locomotion state-machines, and spatial audio to meet the publishing guidelines of Valve's Steam Store.

UNREAL ENGINE 5 RELEASE

Whisper of Anima

Developed by ICAT Game Studio (Chiang Mai University)

A visually stunning third-person fantasy action game built entirely in Unreal Engine by Chiang Mai University students. The project showcases advanced graphics rendering pipelines, locomotion blending, and performance optimization benchmarking industry-grade student capability.
